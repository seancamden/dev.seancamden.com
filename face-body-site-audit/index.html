Upgrade jQuery

jQuery's current version (as of May, 2019) is 3.4.1 and face-body.com is using 1.11.2. According to jQuery's upgrade guide, we are just over the cutoff of 1.11.0 or older, and so jQuery's migration plugin should have us covered.

This one's a little tricky to test, though. We might want to pick a slow sales period to do this.

Nav Bar

In addition to improving color contrast (as explained below in the Colors section), the following issues deserve remediation:

  • Skip to content button should be added. (A)
    Bypass Blocks, Understanding SC 2.4.1 at w3.org
  • A label should be added to the input field. (A)
    Labels or Instructions, Understanding SC 3.3.2 at w3.org
  • A Submit button should be added to the search field.(A)
    H32: Providing submit buttons at w3.org and Digital Accessibility: Creating Inclusive Experiences and Avoiding Lawsuits at UXPAMagazine.org
  • Focus indication should be improved. (AA)
    Focus Visible: Understanding SC 2.4.7 at w3.org
  • Increase font size to 24px as per discussion.

Homepage

Homepage Carousel

I wasn't able to confirm that the homepage carousel, which is part of the District Shopify theme, is accessible. I suspect it is not. Options for correcting that are:

  • Modifying it to add accessibility features
  • Replacing it with an accessible carousel
  • Deleting it

Each solution presents its own problems, of course. Modification would be overly time-consuming. Replacing would probably take less time, but would still be around 8 to 16 hours and would not be guaranteed to be visually and functionally identical. Deleting it would probably make the most sense from a purely practical perspective, but might be unnatractive for other reasons.

Here are some examples of accessible carousels:

  • Web Accessibility Tutorials: Carousels at w3.org
  • Accessibility Oz

Because text over images presents additional challenges to achieving sufficient contrast, I recommend the font colors used in the carousel be either black or white. Another option is giving text a contrast-helping background color as depicted in the w3 carousel link above.

Homepage Copy and Images

The lines of copy: "NEED HELP CHOOSING YOUR NUMBING PRODUCTS? VIEW THE CHART." and "INSURANCE FOR YOUR TATTOO SHOP or SALON: CLICK HERE" should be reworded, "FOR HELP CHOOSING YOUR NUMBING PRODUCTS, VIEW THE CHART." and "CLICK HERE FOR INSURANCE FOR YOUR TATTOO SHOP or SALON" respectively.

The two images below the carousel have two links each, which is redundant. H2: Combining adjacent image and text links for the same resource at w3.org (A)
Their structure should be modified to use only one link.

Colors (AA)

Webaim Color Contrast Checker

Colors in Shopify can mostly be controlled from within the admin panel. In some areas of the face-body.com theme, direct control has been defeated either by accident or design, but putting things right should not take more than two to four hours.

Here are the areas I spotted that should be changed:

  • Change highlight color from
    #24A9A1/#34b0b0 to #278282
  • Change font color from
    #938c8c to #595454
  • See Homepage section for additional recommendations for text in the carousels.
